Fuel Cell Vehicle

This project involves the simulation of a fuel cell vehicle using Simscape, a MATLAB tool used for modeling and simulating physical systems. The primary goal is to model the dynamics and control of a fuel cell-powered vehicle, including its powertrain and associated systems. The project contains various components, such as models, scripts, and data files that collectively represent the fuel cell vehicle's operation.

Overview:

  • Simulates the dynamics and control of a fuel cell-powered vehicle using MATLAB and Simulink.
  • Models the powertrain, including the fuel cell stack, electric motor, and battery.
  • Aims to optimize vehicle performance and efficiency under different operating conditions.

Technologies:

  • MATLAB & Simulink: For modeling and simulation.
  • Simscape: For physical system modeling.
  • XML: For configuration data and simulation results.

Methodology:

  • Develop Simulink models to represent the vehicle's powertrain components.
  • Use MATLAB scripts for parameter initialization, simulation execution, and result analysis.
  • Perform simulations under various scenarios to evaluate performance and efficiency.

Key Contributions:

  • Created detailed models for the fuel cell stack, electric motor, and battery.
  • Automated the simulation process with MATLAB scripts.
  • Analyzed simulation results to provide insights into vehicle behavior and optimization.

Outcome:

  • Achieved a comprehensive understanding of fuel cell vehicle dynamics and control.
  • Identified key parameters affecting performance and efficiency.
  • Provided a robust simulation framework for future fuel cell vehicle development.
